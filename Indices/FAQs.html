Technical Support

  • A game won't launch — what should I do?

    First, try restarting the platform and your computer. If the issue persists, verify the game files through your library, update your graphics drivers, and make sure your system meets the minimum requirements for the game.

  • What are the minimum system requirements?

    System requirements vary from game to game. You can find the specific requirements listed on each game's store page before purchasing or downloading.

  • The game is crashing — how do I fix it?

    Try the following steps: update your graphics drivers, lower the in-game graphics settings, close any background applications, and verify the game files. If the problem continues, visit our support page and submit a report with your crash log.
