Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about your Game Sphere account and library.

How do I download my games?

Go to your Library by clicking the book icon in the navigation bar. Select your game and click the "Install" button.

Can I share my library with friends?

Game Sphere currently supports Family Sharing for up to 5 accounts within the same household.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and Game Sphere Gift Cards.

What are the system requirements?

Each game page has a 'Requirements' section. We recommend checking these before purchasing to ensure smooth gameplay.

Can I get a refund?

Yes, as long as the request is within 14 days of purchase and the game has been played for less than 2 hours.

How do I change my username?

Navigate to your Profile and click 'Edit Profile' to update your display name and avatar.

Is Game Sphere available on mobile?

Currently, Game Sphere is optimized for desktop browsers, but you can browse the shop on your mobile device.

Can I play games offline?

Many single-player titles support offline mode. You just need to be online once to verify the initial launch.

How do I secure my account?

We highly recommend enabling Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) in your Settings page.

Where can I see my purchase history?

Click on your username in the top left and select 'Account Details' to see all previous transactions.

How do I contact support?

If you encounter any bugs, use the 'Contact Us' link in the footer to open a support ticket with our team.
