Detroit: Become Human

Developer: Quantic Dream     Publisher: Sony Interactive Entertainment

Released: May 25, 2018

Click outside the image to close

Detroit: Become Human is a narrative adventure set in a near-future Detroit where androids have become part of everyday life. You control three android characters — Kara, Connor, and Markus — each facing a different path as they question their existence and fight for their place in the world. Every choice you make shapes branching storylines, leading to dramatically different outcomes. With stunning visuals and a deeply human story, this is one of the most cinematic games ever made.
